Gävle Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Purpose

Keeping the Nordic Spirit alive, the Gävle chamber of commerce aim to broaden its horizons and bring supply to the current demand of the people. Memberships will see cooperation from business deals to first hand views of developed areas.

Scenarios

  • What will we do about the decline of ships in the harbor?
  • How can we profit the most?
  • What can be changed because of the increase in population?

Color Schema

The blue color is to be used in smaller backgrounds, with the yellow highlighting important text or titles.
