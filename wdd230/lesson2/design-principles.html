Visual Hierarchy

Standing Stone Games

LOTRO

This website clearly showcase the visual hierarchy principle. It does so by placing the front person with the flame in higher contrast, lighting up his face. Directly behind there is a figure in gray, making a visual perspective to the sharp front. Going from front to back there are stops where your eyes go.

Contrast

Nintendo America

Nintendo

The biggest contrast in this picture are the colors. Against the white background on the right side the rest of the colors pop out. The strong red and blue pull you in to them, while the green give variety. While the background is in chaos, the main title is white, making it a good contrast with each other, and it's easy to distinguish.

Rule of Thirds

Best Friends Sanctuary

Best Friends

Specifically the tree and ground make up the rule of third in this picture. The human and dog is slightly more to the right, following again the rule. It is more pleasing to the eye because the subjects and objects are placed according to the rule of thirds, it leaves the other two thirds open.
