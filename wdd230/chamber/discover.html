Demographic

Sweden has a population of over 102,000 people as of 2020, with more growth to come in future years. Gävle is as big as 1,613 km2 and is situated by the Baltic Sea. It has the same latitude as Helsinki and the same longtitude as Vienna and Cape Town. Summers can be really pleasant with 25 degrees celsius, while the winter can be bone biting cold, reaching temperatures as low as -30.
