Current Events

Decorating For Christmas

On December 1 we will be decorating the city for Christmas! Gold and Silver members have the ability and freedom to help decorate a specific part of town which in turn will help draw attention to their businesses. The Commerce happily pay for any decorations needed to make Christmas special for everyone in Gävle.

The Gävle Goat

On the 3rd of December at 8pm we will celebrate the Gävle Goat by the city library. Fireworks, history, and music will be available to enjoy. Don't miss it! For more information, please see Gävle Goat

Founding Of The Commerce

On the 20th of December we will hold our annual celebration of the Commerces founding. In 1446 Gävle was officially declared a city, and is the oldest in Swedens Northern Lands. We will honor the dedication that business owners throughout the centuries gave to Gävle, making it a city of industry and fame. Keeping the Northern Spirit alive, we will give everyone who attend a commemorative coin of the occasion.

Baseball Tournament

On the 30th of December, the Gävle Goats Baseball Club will hold their annual winter baseball tournament. Hosted by Pro players Pierre, Vincent, Alex, and Peter, they want to keep the city warm with shouts and praising for whatever team onlookers will be cheering for. But of course most of the cheering will go to Gävle. To enroll your team and secure a spot in the greatest event of the year, please see Thomas for details.
